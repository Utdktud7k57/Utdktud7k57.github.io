Discovering Prognostic Biomarkers in Cancer

My Master’s dissertation applied clustering algorithms to high-dimensional gene expression data to uncover potential prognostic biomarkers in cancer. The study compared K-Means, Hierarchical Clustering, and DBSCAN while evaluating performance using validation indices. Visualizations were developed to bridge computational outcomes with biological interpretation.

Distinguishing Fraudulent Reviews from Genuine Ones

A literature review investigating detection methods for fake online reviews in e-commerce and social media. It compared content-based techniques (NLP and ML for linguistic anomalies) with network-based methods (graph and social network analysis). The findings emphasized that a hybrid approach could enhance accuracy in combating deceptive opinion spam.
